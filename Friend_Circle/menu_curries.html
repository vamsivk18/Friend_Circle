Chicken Curry

A fiery Andhra curry is cooked with ginger-garlic paste, curry leaves, cinnamon, chicken masala and a delectable cashew and poppy seed paste. Pair with crispy appams or rice!

Bendakaya Pulusu

Love okra? This absolutely delicious curry of okra in tangy tamarind gravy is a perfect dish from the Andhra region to try next.

Gongura Momsam

A mind-blowing meat delicacy, gongura mamsam is a hearty mix of cinnamon, cardamom, onions, garlic and chilli with gongura leaves and lamb, this one is a meat lovers' delight!

Prawn Curry
