Soft Drinks


Coco Cola Classic

Coca Cola Classic is the world's most popular caffeinated soft drink and it is made by the Coca Cola Company, one of the largest companies in the world

Mountain Dew

Mountain Dew is a popular citrus-flavored carbonated drink - stylized as Mtn Dew, and manufactured by PepsiCo.

Dr Peppers

Dr Pepper is a popular soft drink made with 23 flavors to a secret recipe, the oldest major brand in the U.S. Contains 150 calories per 12oz can

Sprite

Sprite is an American-owned brand of clear, lemon and lime-flavored soft drink created by The Coca-Cola Company. Sprite comes in multiple flavors, including cranberry, cherry, grape, orange, tropical, ginger, and vanilla, and newer versions of the drinks are artificially sweetened.
